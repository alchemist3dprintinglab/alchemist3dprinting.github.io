Materials & Capabilities

FDM 3D printing suitable for functional parts, brackets, enclosures, mounts and visual prototypes.

Materials

Common options are:

  • PLA – general purpose, good detail, low warp
  • PETG – stronger, temperature & impact resistant
  • ABS – more heat resistant
  • PA – more flixible
  • CF – more structural strength

Quality Options

Choose between faster prints or finer surface finish depending on your requirement and budget.
